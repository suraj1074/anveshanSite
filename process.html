Anveshan Process

  • Crop Selector

    Help farmers decide which crop to sow based on factors like soil quality and water availability

  • Crop Monitoring

    Monitoring the growth of crops to ensure high quality and maximum yield using technology

  • Traditional Methods

    Using traditional methods evolved over generations in our very own farms and villages to deliver high-quality products

  • Building Awareness

    Helping consumers identify the unhealthy parts of their diet and replace them with the healthiest options available.

Firstly, we focus on educating farmers on scientific farming and we enable them to make data-driven decisions. Crop selection is the key and data from the soil, weather forecast and market trends must be considered before taking a decision.

Selecting the right crop plays a crucial role in affecting the quality and yield of the final product.

Soil

The composition of soil and presence of natural nutrients is analysed. This helps to decide which crop to grow for maximum yield.

Temperature

The temperature conditions affect plant growth. A crop that is fit for the temperature profile must be chosen.

Weather

Over 70% of Indian farms are rain-fed. Having an accurate prediction of weather is key in ascertaining the water availability of the land.

We provide technology solutions to the problems that farmers face while growing their crops. Be it pests, lack of nutrition or plant diseases we have got the answers.

Active crop monitoring can reduce total crop loss by 40%, effectively solving India's food independence.

Drones and satellite imagery

The bird’s eye view from drones and large scale images from satellites can reveal irrigation problems, soil variation and pest infestation.

Crop Doctors

Consultant support for the farmer to tackle issues such as pest infection and plant diseases. Effective advice to control damage available directly.

Nutrient Analyser

Based on the analysis of plant health using satellite images and drones, we provide the nutrients plant needs at different stages before harvest.

Using the wisdom of past generations to create premium quality products for today's generation.

Bilona Ghee

Curd-churned or Bilona ghee is of primary importance in Ayurveda. It is the same ghee Krishna was very fond of as a young child.

Wood Pressed Oils

Pressing oilseeds at low temperatures using the cold-pressed process to preserve naturally occurring nutrients.

Hand Crafted

Our products come from small farms. Each is handcrafted and packed with love and care from the villages of India.
