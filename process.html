Firstly, we focus on educating farmers on scientific farming and we enable them to make data-driven decisions. Crop selection is the key and data from the soil, weather forecast and market trends must be considered before taking a decision.

Selecting the right crop plays a crucial role in affecting the quality and yield of the final product.

Soil

The composition of soil and presence of natural nutrients is analysed. This helps to decide which crop to grow for maximum yield.

Temperature

The temperature conditions affect plant growth. A crop that is fit for the temperature profile must be chosen.

Weather

Over 70% of Indian farms are rain-fed. Having an accurate prediction of weather is key in ascertaining the water availability of the land.
