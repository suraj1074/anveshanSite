Groundnut oil, also referred to as peanut oil or arachis oil, is a type of vegetable oil commonly used in cooking that it derived from peanuts. Groundnut oil comes in a number of varieties like refined, unrefined, roasted and cold-pressed but they all differ in terms of their nutritional and health benefits. Buy Now

Sesame oil is an edible vegetable oil derived from sesame seeds. Besides being used as a cooking oil in South India, it is used as a flavour enhancer in Middle Eastern, African, and Southeast Asian cuisines. It has a distinctive nutty aroma and taste. The oil is used for cooking and is one of the earliest-known crop-based oils. Worldwide mass modern production is limited due to the inefficient manual harvesting process required to extract the oil. The impressive amount of vitamins such as vitamin E and vitamin K, organic compounds, and other beneficial components in Cold Pressed Sesame Oil is the reason why it is considered a healthy option compared to many other oils.

Key Features

  • Carefully extracted from the best-quality sesame seeds using the advanced cold pressed method.
  • Sesame oil is rich in essential Omega-6 fatty acids which reduces the risk of heart diseases.
  • Sesame oil serves as a cure for fungal skin diseases and glowy skin.
  • Ayurveda claims it to be the ideal choice for oil-pulling, strengthening teeth and bones.
